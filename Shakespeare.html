Who was William Shakespaere?

59.000 VND

Author: Jess Brailler, Albert.A Parker


Description

Shakespeare's cherished dramas continue to be staged globally, yet the existence of the planet's most renowned playwright remains predominantly enigmatic. A youthful William departed from Stratford to follow a theatrical path in London, where his creations eventually flourished, bestowing upon him fame and affluence. Through monochrome illustrations, which encompass a blueprint of the renowned Globe theater, Celeste Davidson Mannis assembles the fragments of Shakespeare's life and creative output for young audiences.
